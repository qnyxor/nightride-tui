Notes for downstream users
--------------------------

- The grant above is permissive ("100% free for personal and
  commercial use") but is *not* an OSI-approved or SPDX-listed font
  license. Treat it as a custom permissive grant from the author.
- The font is bundled with nightride-tui purely as an installable
  brand asset for users who want the Nightride FM display face on
  their system. The TUI itself does not require this font at
  runtime — it ships with Iosevka Term Nerd Font Regular for the
  rasterised UI.
- Author and ownership claims belong to Z (creator of Nightride FM,
  z@nightride.fm). nightride-tui redistributes the font with
  attribution; the upstream README is preserved verbatim alongside
  the asset at `assets/NightrideFMMonospace.LICENSE.txt`.
